Alexander "ALX" Atkins is a 23 year old creative with a bi-coastal presence in New York City and Los Angeles. and a strong interest in creative direction, video production, music, fashion, and artist development.

His passion and knowledge for all things pop culture (music, art, videos, artists, directors, producers, fashion, digital and current trends). drive him to execute all creative, marketing, and operational tasks needed to aid talent and executives and produce potent art.
